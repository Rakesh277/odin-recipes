Veggie Biryani

Description

This Veg Biryani is an authentic Indian vegetarian recipe packed full of your favorite rice, veggies, and spices. Made with patience and lots of love

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Water
  • Mix Veggies
  • Lemon (optional)
  • Biryani Masala

How to make Veggie Biryani

  1. Soak the rice
  2. Fry onions in oil until transparent
  3. Put some Masala once onion are fried
  4. Add veggies and cook until soften
  5. Add water, double of rice usually
  6. Add soaked rice
  7. Set the stove high for five minutes until water is fully absorbed
  8. Let the lid be closed and rice cook on steam generated inside for at least 15 minutes
  9. that's it. Congratulations on successfully making veggie Biryani
