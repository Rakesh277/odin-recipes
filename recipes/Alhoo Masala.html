Alhoo Masala

Description

An easy one-pot potato curry packed with so many flavours. Soft, tender potatoes slathered in a spicy, onion masala. If you want a delicious dinner on the table in no more than an hour, then this recipe is perfect.

Ingredients list

  • 500 g Potatoes
  • 50 ml Olive Oil
  • 2 Onions
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Chilli Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds Jeera
  • 2 tsp Dried Fenugreek Leaves Methi
  • 2 Green Chillies
  • 100 ml Water
  • Coriander

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in pan and sauté cumin seeds for a few minutes until fragrant
  2. Add then diced onions and cook on medium heat for 4-5 minutes until softened
  3. Add the onions are cooking chop the tomatoes then add into pan before cooking for 5 minutes until the tomatoes are soft
  4. dd chilli powder, salt and turmeric powder then cook the spices for a few minutes – add 2 tablespoons of then water to ensure that the spices do not burn and to give a more intense colour
  5. Add the spices are cooking peel and chop the potatoes then add into the pan along with the water and cook for 15-20 minutes
  6. Add the dried fenugreek leaves, chopped green chillies and garam masala and cook for 2-3 minutes
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander
  8. Serve immediately with fresh homemade naan
